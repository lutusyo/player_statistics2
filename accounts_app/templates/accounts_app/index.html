What is this platform about?

Azam FC Academy Data Analysis Platform is a modern performance intelligence tool tailored for youth development. It integrates tactical breakdowns, Catapult GPS data, and individual match stats to empower coaches, players, and analysts with meaningful insights.

Our mission is to improve player performance through smart data interpretation, long-term tracking, and contextualized football analytics.
